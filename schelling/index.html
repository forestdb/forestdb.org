Schelling coordination game

Two agents, Alice and Bob, want to meet. They choose which bar to go to by recursively reasoning about one another (Schelling 1960).

(define (sample-location)
  (if (flip .55)
      'popular-bar
      'unpopular-bar))

(define (alice depth)
  (query
   (define alice-location (sample-location))
   alice-location
   (equal? alice-location (bob (- depth 1)))))

(define (bob depth)
  (query
   (define bob-location (sample-location))
   bob-location
   (or (= depth 0)
       (equal? bob-location (alice depth)))))

Schelling, Thomas C. 1960. The Strategy of Conflict. Harvard University Press.
